Waldo Geraldo Faldo

Manager at Family Matters

Waldo Geraldo Faldo (born 1970s) was portrayed by actor Shawn Harrison and part of Family Matters from 1990 to 1996.In Season 2 and 3,he was a reccuring character.From Season 4 to Season 7,he was a main character.

For most of the time Waldo was featured on the show, he was friends with Eddie Winslow (the son of the show's main characters, Carl and Harriette Winslow) and the Winslows' quintessential nerdy and bothersome neighbor, Steve Urkel .
